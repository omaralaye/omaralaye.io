How Our Smart Technology Works for Your Farm Our pig health monitoring system, powered by the ESP32-CAM and a user-friendly Flutter app, uses smart technology trained on over 200 pig pictures to detect if your livestock is healthy or sick. The graphs above show how well it’s learning, based on 80 training sessions (called "epochs"). Here’s what it means for you! The Accuracy Graph tracks how often the system gets it right. The blue line shows it starts at 40% and climbs to 80-90% with the pictures it practiced on—proof it’s getting sharp at spotting health issues in familiar pigs! The orange line, for new pictures it hasn’t seen, starts at 30-40% and settles around 50-60%. This gap tells us it’s better with pigs it knows, but we’re working to make it just as good with every pig on your farm. The Loss Graph shows how much the system struggles to learn. The blue line drops from 1.6 to 0.2-0.4, meaning it’s growing confident with the practice pictures. The orange line falls from 1.6 to 0.8-1.0 but wiggles a bit, showing it needs help with surprises. This suggests the system is a bit too focused on what it already knows—something like recognizing a pig you’ve seen before but missing a new one. We’re improving it by adding more pictures with different lighting and pig types, plus fine-tuning to catch every health issue early. This means healthier pigs, less worry, and more time for you to focus on your farm. Ready to see it in action? Learn How to Use or Explore More about how this technology can work for you!

How to Use the Pig Disease Detection System

Step 1: Switch On the ESP32-CAM

  • Power on the ESP32-CAM device using the power supply.
  • Ensure the device is connected to Wi-Fi network for streaming.

Step 2: Launch the Mobile App

  • Open the pig disease detection app on your mobile phone.
  • Connect the app to the ESP32-CAM stream by entering the device IP address.

Step 3: Start Streaming and Detecting

  • Start the live video stream from the ESP32-CAM on the app.
  • The app analyzes the video feed to detect pig health status.
  • View real-time detection results such as “Healthy” or “Sick” labels.

Step 4: Monitor and Take Action

  • Receive notifications for any detected sick pigs.
  • Take appropriate action based on the detection results.
  • Troubleshoot common issues like connectivity or camera errors if needed.
